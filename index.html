Introduction to E-stim

This is a beginner-friendly guide that explains how to administer electrical stimulation (e-stim) therapy using a multi-modality console. These instructions are designed for Sports Medicine Interns at UC Davis with little to no prior electrotherapy experience.

Purpose of This Guide

This guide teaches beginner users how to administer electrotherapy treatments through a series of step-by-step topics. Users will learn how to initialize the console, place electrode pads, modify parameters, and set up therapeutic currents (IFC, Premod, Combo).

Who This Guide is For

  • New interns with little or no clinical experience
  • Experienced interns who want a quick review of system interface basics

  • How do I turn on E-stim?
    How to turn on the E-stim machine to conduct clinical electrical stimulation therapy.
  • How do I select or modify channels?
    How to toggle between channels to adjust independent treatment zones.
