How do I select or modify channels?

How to toggle between channels to adjust independent treatment zones.

  • The device must be on and resting on the home menu.
  • Lead wires must be plugged into the target channel ports.
Multi-modality therapy units allow clinicans to treat multiple areas or multiple patients at the same time using different output channels. Modifying specific channels allows you to adjust parameters for one targeted muscle group or joint without disrupting active treatments running on other channels.
  1. Identify "Select Channel" on display screen and the physical button bordering it.
  2. Press that physical button bordering the screen interface to select a channel.
    Note: Pressing the physical button will change the channels. Keep pressing until the desired channel is outlined.
  3. Select electrical stimulation therapy to conduct the modality.
The selected channel will appear outlined on the console, and now you can adjust the treatment for that specific channel.
